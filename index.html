Services We Provide

From kitchens to basements, flooring to finish carpentry—we handle renovations, repairs, and light construction across the GTA.

Kitchen, Bathroom & Full-Home Renovations

Complete transformations—from layout design and demolition to custom cabinetry, countertops, fixtures, and final finishes.

We coordinate every trade, manage permits, and keep you informed throughout. Whether it's a modern kitchen upgrade, a spa-inspired bathroom, or a whole-home refresh, we deliver clean, timely, and durable results.

Basement Finishing & Legal Secondary Suites

Turn unused space into livable area. We handle framing, insulation, drywall, flooring, trim, and permit applications for legal suites.

Our basement builds include proper egress, ceiling height compliance, moisture control, and electrical/plumbing coordination with licensed trades. We navigate city requirements so your investment is safe and rental-ready.

Flooring: Tile, Vinyl, Hardwood

Professional installation of ceramic, porcelain, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), and engineered or solid hardwood. Subfloor prep included.

We assess moisture, level subfloors, and ensure proper underlayment. Precision cuts, clean grout lines, and smooth transitions between rooms. Your floors will look great and last.

Drywall, Taping & Painting

Smooth walls and ceilings, seamless taping, and professional paint application. Repairs, patches, texture matching, and full room repaints.

We prep surfaces properly, apply quality primers and paints, and protect your furnishings. Clean lines, even coats, and a fresh finish that enhances your space.

Carpentry & Trim Work

Custom baseboards, crown moulding, door and window casings, wainscoting, built-ins, shelving, and feature/accent walls.

Precision miters, tight joints, and quality materials. We can match existing trim or install modern profiles. Whether it's a fireplace surround or a full-room trim package, we deliver craftsmanship you'll notice.

Tiling, Backsplashes, Showers & Waterproofing

Expert tile layout and installation for floors, walls, backsplashes, and wet areas. Proper waterproofing membranes and schluter systems.

We use modern waterproofing techniques (Kerdi, RedGard) to protect against moisture. Clean grout lines, level tiles, and durable seals. Your shower or kitchen backsplash will perform and look stunning.

Framing, Minor Repairs & Handyman Items

Structural framing for additions, load-bearing adjustments, plus punch-list and handyman tasks like door adjustments, drywall repairs, fixture replacements.

Need a wall removed or a new opening framed? We handle structural changes with proper support and permits. Also available for smaller fixes—no job too small when it fits our schedule.

Exterior Upgrades: Decks, Fences, Porches

Seasonal outdoor projects including deck builds and repairs, fence installation, porch construction, and exterior trim. Coordination with licensed trades for electrical and plumbing as required.

We build with pressure-treated lumber, composite decking, or cedar. Proper footings, level framing, and durable fasteners. When your project needs an electrician or plumber, we bring in trusted, licensed professionals.

Licensed subcontractors engaged as required. We coordinate electricians and plumbers when your project needs them.
